Conclusion & Future Roadmap

Project Summary

Speaking English with AI addresses a critical gap in language learning by providing:

AI-powered conversation practice available 24/7

Real speech processing with transcription and feedback

Personalized learning through mistake pattern analysis

Realistic role-plays for practical language scenarios

Targeted improvement through detailed feedback

Key Achievement: We've created a comprehensive solution that combines cutting-edge AI technology with proven language learning methodologies to create an effective, accessible platform for English speaking practice.
